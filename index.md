## About Me
My name is Coleton and I am a PhD student in Anthropology at Brown University as well as a graduate of National Dong Hwa University's College of Indigenous Studies in Taiwan (東華大學原民會-族群關係). I am a lingusitic anthropologist whose work focuses on language revitalization and reclamation in Taiwan, especially among the Pangcah/'Amis. I am interested in GenAI and its role in data colonialism, entanglements between the open-source and language activist communities, as well as digital ethnographic and decolonial methodologies more broadly.

My master's thesis is _Mipaselak to sowal no Pangcah: a digital ethnography of two open 'Amis language platforms, 'Amis MoeDict and 'Amis Wikipitiya_ and is available via the NDHU Library.
