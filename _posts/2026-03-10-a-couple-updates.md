This news is now slightly delayed, but I am excited to announce that I was awarded the Critical Language Scholarship for Chinese! This scholarship will allow me to attend an 8-week intensive Chinese program in Tainan, Taiwan. I love Tainan but have only ever visited as a tourist, so am very excited to go and get a little of the (very hot) experience of living there.

Another bit of good news is that I will be presenting at the McGill East Asian Studies Graduate Symposium from April 22-23. The symposium's theme is "Margins, Metaphor and Medium: Subaltern Voices in East Asia." It looks like there are quite a few great presentations lined up, and I am looking forward to attending with a few other researchers from Brown. Montreal has been on my list of places to visit since moving to the Northeast, so I am super excited to attend this April.

The next few months will fly by with this and all the wedding planning. All great things though!
